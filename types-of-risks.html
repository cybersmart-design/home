Types of Risks & Threats

Understand the most common cybersecurity risks and learn how to protect yourself online.

Start Learning

Malware

Malware is harmful software such as viruses and ransomware.
Solution: Keep software updated, use antivirus tools, and back up data.

Phishing

Fake emails or websites trick users into revealing sensitive data.
Solution: Check links carefully and enable two-factor authentication.

Data Breaches

Unauthorized access to private information.
Solution: Use strong passwords and monitor accounts regularly.

Social Engineering

Manipulation techniques used to trick people.
Solution: Verify identities and never share sensitive info unexpectedly.

Denial of Service (DoS)

Attacks that overload systems and cause downtime.
Solution: Use firewalls and traffic monitoring tools.
