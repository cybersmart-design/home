Log 02: Classified Threats

Threat Intelligence

01. Malware

Short for "Malicious Software." Digital infections designed to hijack files, steal data, or damage entire operating systems.

COUNTERMEASURE: Anti-Virus + System Patching

02. Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity in electronic communications.

COUNTERMEASURE: Verify Sender + MFA

03. Data Breach

Security incidents where sensitive, protected, or confidential data is copied, transmitted, or viewed by an unauthorized individual.

COUNTERMEASURE: End-to-End Encryption

04. Social Engineering

The art of manipulating people into divulging confidential information or performing actions that compromise security.

COUNTERMEASURE: Identity Verification

05. DoS Attack

Denial-of-Service attacks overwhelm a system's resources, making it unavailable to legitimate users by flooding it with traffic.

COUNTERMEASURE: Firewall + Traffic Filtering
