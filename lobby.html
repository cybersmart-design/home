What is CyberSmart?

CyberSmart is a cybersecurity awareness platform designed to help individuals understand online threats and protect themselves.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from attacks like hacking, phishing, and malware.

Why Awareness is Important

Many cyber attacks succeed because users are unaware of threats. Awareness helps people avoid scams and risks.

Why It Matters

Cybersecurity protects personal information, finances, and identity. Staying informed keeps communities safe.
