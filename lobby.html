CyberSmart teaches you the skills to understand, prevent, and respond to digital risks. Start by learning the theory, then test your knowledge in challenge-style simulations.

Why Cybersecurity Knowledge Matters

Every device, account, and online interaction carries potential risks. Understanding these threats empowers you to make safer choices and protect your digital life.

Protect Your Data

Learn how breaches occur and how sensitive data can be exposed — and how to prevent it.

Recognize Scams

Understand phishing and other online scams so you can identify threats before they impact you.

Prevent Malware

Discover malware types, how they spread, and steps to secure your devices.

Maintain Privacy

Learn how personal data is collected, tracked, and how to safeguard your digital footprint.

Practical Solutions & Best Practices

Knowledge alone isn’t enough — CyberSmart teaches actionable steps you can apply to stay safe online.

Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords and use password managers to secure accounts.

Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra security layer for accounts to prevent unauthorized access.

Safe Browsing

Learn to spot suspicious links, check website legitimacy, and avoid risky downloads.

Device & Network Security

Keep devices updated, use firewalls, and secure Wi-Fi connections.

Quick Tips & Resources

Stay proactive in your cybersecurity journey with small steps every day.

Regular Updates

Keep your software, browsers, and apps up to date to patch vulnerabilities.

Think Before You Click

Always verify links and attachments before interacting.

Back Up Data

Maintain offline or cloud backups to recover from attacks or failures.

Stay Informed

Follow reputable cybersecurity news sources to keep up with new threats.
