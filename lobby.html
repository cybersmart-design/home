What is CyberSmart?

CyberSmart is a cybersecurity awareness platform designed to help individuals understand online threats and protect themselves in today’s digital world.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from attacks like hacking, phishing, and malware to keep information safe.

Why Awareness is Important

Many cyber attacks succeed because users are unaware of threats. Awareness helps recognize risks, avoid scams, and make safer online choices.

Why It Matters

Cybersecurity protects personal information, finances, and identities. Staying informed safeguards yourself, your family, and your community.
