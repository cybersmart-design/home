About CyberSmart

A research-based cybersecurity simulation designed to evaluate and improve phishing detection awareness through controlled, realistic email scenario testing.

🎯

Objective

This simulation serves as a formal research instrument to measure participants' ability to identify sophisticated phishing attempts. The platform provides a controlled environment for data collection on threat detection patterns, decision-making behaviors, and the effectiveness of structured educational frameworks in improving cybersecurity awareness. Unlike gamified platforms, this simulation prioritizes realistic email interfaces and systematic evaluation over entertainment mechanics.

science

Research Methodology

Participants interact with authentic email simulations that replicate real-world phishing tactics. Each decision is recorded for analysis of recognition accuracy, response time, and confidence levels. Data is anonymized for research purposes.

insights

Key Metrics

The simulation tracks detection accuracy, false positive rates, and response patterns across multiple threat categories including domain spoofing, urgency tactics, and social engineering attempts.

psychology

Educational Framework

Based on established cybersecurity training protocols, the simulation incorporates real-time feedback and indicator breakdowns to reinforce learning without gamification elements.

verified_user

Data Privacy

All simulation data is collected exclusively for academic research purposes. No personally identifiable information is stored. Participants remain anonymous throughout the study.

description This simulation was developed as part of a formal research thesis on cybersecurity awareness, phishing detection methodologies, and the effectiveness of structured educational interventions. The platform serves as a data collection instrument for academic analysis.
